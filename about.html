About Us

Geocatalog is working presently for imparting technical knowledge in the field of Geospatial Technology and Research and Development with a vision of creating utopian quality of life, in the dystopian era. Geocatalog aims to create the legacy of everyone towards sustainability, prosperity, advocacy, equality, environmental protection and self-dependency.

We are working in the following areas:

  • 1. Hands-on training on statistics, GIS, RS, GPS, Photogrammetry and Planning based softwares.
  • 2. Content Development.
  • 3. Academic Writing.
  • 4. Research Consultancy.
  • 5. Online/Offline teaching with customized courses.
